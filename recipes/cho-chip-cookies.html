Chocolate Chip Cookies

If you want to enjoy some delicous chocolate chip cookies look no further. This recipe will show you step-by-step how to make soft and crispy chocolate chip cookies. This recipe serves 24, with a total time of 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teasoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate

Step-by-Step

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips. Place on baking pan with parchment paper.
  3. Bake for about 10 mins in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
