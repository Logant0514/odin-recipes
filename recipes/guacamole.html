Guacamole

A simple and quick way to make guacamole. Servings 16, total time to make 40 mins.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and peppper to taste

Step-by-Step

  1. Peel and mash avacados in a medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, garlic, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Season with remaining lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill for half an hour to blend flavors.
